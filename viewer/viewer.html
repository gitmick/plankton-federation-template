ⓘ about⚙
A provenance graph, assembled in your browser from one or more plankton/nekton registries (federated by content hash). Round nodes are fotons (recorded computations), squares are claims (signed statements about them); edges are lineage (builds-on) and attestation (about, reproduces, reviewed-by). Every node's signature is re-verified here (WASM, Ed25519/DSSE) against the registry's published keys - the record-authentic tier. Click a record to walk its pedigree; click a file to preview its bytes, re-hashed on the spot to confirm they match. Verify with a non-kton tool: kton example 13; re-derive the bytes: example 03. Esc or browser Back returns to the overview.
